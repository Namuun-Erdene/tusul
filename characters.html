Hashiras

Kagaya Ubuyashiki (産屋敷 耀哉, Ubuyashiki Kagaya) is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is the invalid but brilliant leader of the Demon Slayer Corps as well as 95th of his family line. He is voiced by Toshiyuki Morikawa (Japanese) and Matthew Mercer (English).

Demons

Muzan Kibutsuji (鬼き舞ぶ辻つじ 無む惨ざん Kibutsuji Muzan?) is the main antagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is the Demon King, the first of his kind, and the progenitor of all other demons in existence. [8] Muzan is also the leader of the Twelve Kizuki, an organization of the twelve strongest demons in existence that serve directly under him

Students

A Tsuguko ( 継 つぐ 子 こ , Tsuguko?, lit. Successor) is an exceptionally talented combatant of the Demon Slayer Corps who is designated as the apprentice and eventual successor of one of the Hashira.
